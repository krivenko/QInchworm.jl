QInchworm.ppgf.operator_product — Method
operator_product(...)

Evaluate a product of vertices at different contour times z_i with the pseudo-particle Green's function sandwiched in between.

vertices is a contour-time ordered list of triples (z_i, c_i, o_i) were: z_i is a contour time, c_i is +1/-1 for creation/annihilation operator respectively, and o_i is a spin-orbital index
